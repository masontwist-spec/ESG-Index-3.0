Methodology

The FTSE 100 ESG Exposure Index is a contrarian framework. Higher values reflect greater commitments, programmes, incentives, reporting intensity, and related governance integration. Lower scores therefore rank higher on the Index.

Environment

  • Climate Targets captures target-setting, scope coverage, and related climate commitments.
  • Investment & Transition captures transition actions, spending, and related climate-oriented commitments.
  • Climate Reporting captures disclosure intensity, KPIs, reporting breadth, and assurance-related indicators.

Social

  • DEI Targets & Representation captures formal targets and representation-related exposure.
  • DEI Programmes & Memberships captures memberships, programmes, and participation indicators.
  • Social Incentives captures incentive structures, oversight, and related social-governance features.

Governance Treatment

  • Governance is not presented as a standalone public-facing scoring domain on the website.
  • Instead, governance-related indicators are embedded within the Environment and Social frameworks, especially in reporting, oversight, and incentive-related measures.
  • This avoids double counting and keeps the public-facing structure easier to interpret.
Public composite model: The website’s public-facing ESG score is currently built from the live Environment and Social modules. Governance-related indicators remain embedded within those domains rather than shown as a standalone scored pillar.

How to Use the Pages

The site is structured so that users can move from the domain-specific views into the combined public-facing composite.

Environment Page

Use the Environment page to inspect the climate-related sub-pillars and the total Environment score.

Social Page

Use the Social page to inspect the Social sub-pillars and the total Social score.

ESG Page

Use the ESG page for the current public-facing composite ranking built from Environment and Social, and the Companies page for a simpler directory-style cross-section of those scores.
