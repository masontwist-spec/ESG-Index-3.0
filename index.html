How to Read the Site

Each page applies the same contrarian interpretation: lower scores rank higher. Use the Environment and Social pages to see domain-specific drivers, and the ESG page for the overall composite ranking.

Environment

The Environment page shows the live climate-related sub-pillars: Climate Targets, Investment & Transition, and Climate Reporting.

Social

The Social page shows DEI Targets & Representation, DEI Programmes & Memberships, and Social Incentives.

ESG

The ESG page shows the current composite total score alongside the Environment and Social scores to make the overall ranking easier to interpret.

Construction note: This is not a traditional ESG “quality” index. It is an ESG exposure / burden index. Under the current framework, higher values indicate greater ESG intensity and lower values rank higher.
