Governance treatment: Governance appears in the methodology through strategy integration, board oversight, transparency, and assurance-related signals. These indicators are tracked and shown here, but are not being used again in the total ESG calculation because of redundancy.

How Governance Is Used

The governance layer is not presented as an equal public-facing domain in the current website. Instead, it functions as a supporting methodological layer that informs how oversight, reporting, strategy integration, and incentive-related structures are understood across the broader index.

Strategy Integration

Covers whether ESG-related themes are integrated into the company’s broader corporate strategy, risk language, or formal organizational commitments.

Board Oversight

Covers board-level integration, governance structures, oversight committees, or other signals that indicate formal governance treatment of ESG-related matters.

Transparency & Assurance

Covers frameworks, disclosure structures, external alignment, and other transparency or assurance-oriented indicators.
